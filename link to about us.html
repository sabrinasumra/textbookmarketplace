    According to tutors, students in Year 3 are always looking to sell their textbooks and students in year 1 are always looking to purchase the same textbooks. It makes sense for the year 3 students to be selling to the year 1s. However there is no channel for this. So we decided to create the TEXTBOOK MARKETPLACE; an online market where alumni can create profiles to sell, and new students can get in contact with sellers.
